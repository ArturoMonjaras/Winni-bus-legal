Privacy Policy for Winni Bus
Last Updated: February 2026

Winni Bus (“the Skill”) is an independent Alexa skill that provides real-time public transit arrival information for Winnipeg Transit.

1. Information We Collect

Winni Bus does not collect or store personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise device location.

The Skill processes the following information solely to provide its functionality:
- Transit stop numbers provided by the user
- Street intersections provided by the user
- Optional favorite stop names created by the user (if enabled)

This information is used only to retrieve transit arrival data and respond to user requests.

2. Data Storage

If the user enables favorites, favorite stop information may be stored using Amazon Alexa’s secure storage services and is associated only with the user’s Alexa account.

Winni Bus does not maintain its own independent database of personal user information.

3. Third-Party Services

Winni Bus retrieves real-time transit information from the Winnipeg Transit public API.

Winni Bus is not affiliated with or endorsed by Winnipeg Transit.

4. Data Sharing

Winni Bus does not sell, rent, trade, or share user information with third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

This Skill is not directed toward children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Security

All data processing occurs within Amazon’s Alexa infrastructure and follows Amazon’s security standards.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Skill after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
